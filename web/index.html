Welcome to Follow

Follow is a vehicle tracking system that lets you know where your car is. It works by placing a hi-tech, GPS powered device in your car and then using this site to track its location.

The Follow web site provides you with several functions.

Use the three links in the navigation panel at the top of the page to access the three screens of the application.

Using Maps you can see the locations Follow GPS has registered. It will also show points that have been determined using the device's connection IP address.

Using Connections you can monitor the network connections the device made while talking to the online service.

Finally, use Logs to oversee information and error messages sent over by the device.

Happy driving.
